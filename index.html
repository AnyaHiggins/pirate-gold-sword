Biography of Nancy Pimental

Nancy Pimental was born on May 31, 1965 in a city called Boston, Massachusetts. Not much is known about Nancy's early childhood. In terms of education she graduated from Someret High School in 1983. Shortly after she atttended Worchester Polytechnic Institute, in which she graduated from the University with a degree in Chemical Engineering! As an adult Nancy Pimental has influnced many of our beloved shows and movies. She has written for movies such as, Bad Actress, Dumb and Dumberer, Prey for Rock & Roll, and a few other movies. In terms of shows she was written for South Park and Shameless, which they are two really good shows that have a large folllowing!
